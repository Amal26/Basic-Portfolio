About Me

Hello! My name is Amal (pronounced as Jamal without the J), which means “hope” in Arabic. I’m twenty-six years old and trying to figure out what I want in life. I was raised in Stockholm, Sweden and moved to Minnesota six years ago, and I’m still getting used to this place. I love to travel and try food from different countries, even though I get dizzy when I eat spicy food. My favorite dish so far is Turkish Kebab with rice.

I love to play and watch soccer, ski, and swim. I also like animals, if they’re not ants or dangerous enough to kill me. When I said I’m still trying to figure out what I want to do, I meant it. I have changed my major at least four times, but I think I finally know which direction I want to go. Hey, at least that’s a step. I’m currently a manager at a daycare. Dealing with kids got me questioning whether I want to have children on my own, but I’ve also learned how to be VERY patient.
